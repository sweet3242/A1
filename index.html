Project Idea

Overview

My project proposal is to develop an app that allows users to swap plants, produce and seedlings. It is aimed at plant enthusiasts and horticulturists. It allows them to conveniently find others in their local area who are also looking to swap or give away plants.

Motivation

The reason I wanted to develop this app was because I enjoy growing house plants and various succulents. However, it can be quite an expensive hobby as buying many plants from a nursery can be pricey. In addition to this, some plants are hard to find in local nurseries and need to be shipped. The app provides a platform for users to trade plants/seedlings/propagations so people can exchange what they have to gain new plants.

Description

Users sign up to create their own profile, under which they are able to list the plants they are willing to swap. Users are able to see other members that are close to them and their listings. They are also able to search for specific listings. Once a user finds a plant listing that they desire, they can send a request through to swap for one of their own plants. The member who receives the swap request can choose to decline it or accept it. If they accept the request, this will allow the users to message either to proceed further with the trade.
Main Features of the App:
  • Individual Profiles: Users would sign up to become members and create their user profile, which includes a username, profile photo and their general location. The profile page would show pictures of the plant listings that they have available to swap. Users will be able to view the profiles of others to see what they have available as well.
  • Map: The app will feature a map allowing users to see other members of the community that are nearby their vicinity.
  • Catalogue and Search: The catalogue would allow users to post their listing under the relevant category. The search function would allow users to find listings that they are after by using keywords.
  • Messaging: After a request for a swap has been accepted. Users are able to message each other to organize the specifics.
  • Forums: Where members can post what they are looking for or other general queries. This can be classed under specific categories such as 'succulents' or 'plant identification'.

Tools and Technologies

Front End: An initial wireframe for the app will be made to flesh out the basic features and format of the app. This can be done using programs such as Sketch. Adobe CC can be used to create the design element of the app that include logos, images, designs of screens. ReactJS and AngularJS can be utilized to assist with creating the user interface.
Back End: The app can be developed more easily using an app builder tool such as WIX. However, this may limit its functionality and so it may be more beneficial to develop from scratch using a free software development kit like Android Studio.
Server: The app will need to be hosted on a server such as Azure or Google Cloud.

Skills Required

To build this app, proficient skills would be needed in programming languages like html, css , javascript and java. In addition to this, the successful creation of this app would also require good design skills and data analysis skills. This would most likely be a large undertaking for an individual and would be accomplished better by a team that includes app designers, front-end developers and back-end developers.

Outcome

This app facilitates the trade of plants, while also helping to connect a community with a shared passion. I believe the app will have a dedicated number of users who are hobbyists and enjoy expanding their inventories. This will help them do so in a way that also benefits each other.
